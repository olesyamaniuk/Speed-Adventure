Game Overview

Speed Adventure throws you into non-stop 3D action with lightning-fast parkour, sharp reflexes, and explosive environments. Sprint through traps, leap across rooftops, and blaze through neon-lit tunnels. Perfect for fans of speed runners and adrenaline-packed platforming!

  • Multiple Environments

How to Play

Tap to jump or double jump
Swipe to slide under or dodge left/right
Collect rings and boosters
Beat level goals and unlock new zones!
